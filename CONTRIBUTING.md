# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the National Governance
Transparency Hub (NGTH).

This repository contains the **foundational charter** of NGTH and its core
platform, the Public Accountability & Performance Platform (PAPP).
As such, contributions are handled conservatively.

---

## Scope of Contributions

This repository accepts contributions that:

- Clarify language or improve precision
- Correct factual, typographical, or formatting issues
- Strengthen transparency, accountability, or auditability principles
- Improve structure or readability without altering intent

This repository does **not** accept contributions that:

- Introduce political advocacy or policy positions
- Propose operational implementations
- Expand the authority of AI or automated systems
- Weaken public-interest safeguards

---

## How to Contribute

At the current stage, contributions are handled by the NGTH Steward(s).

If you wish to suggest a change:

1. Open an issue describing the proposed change and its rationale, **or**
2. Contact the project maintainers through the organizationâ€™s public channels

As the project matures, a more formal contribution and review process
may be introduced.

---

## Review Philosophy

All proposed changes are evaluated against the following criteria:

- Does the change preserve political neutrality?
- Does it strengthen public trust?
- Does it maintain long-term institutional integrity?
- Does it avoid introducing operational or ideological bias?

Clarity and restraint are valued over volume or speed.
