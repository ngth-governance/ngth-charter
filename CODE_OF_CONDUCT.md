# Code of Conduct

The National Governance Transparency Hub (NGTH) is committed to maintaining
a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for public-interest
collaboration.

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within this repository
and related project spaces.

---

## Our Commitment

We commit to fostering an environment that is:

- Respectful of differing perspectives
- Focused on public-interest outcomes
- Free from harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Grounded in good faith and professional conduct

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Engage constructively and respectfully
- Focus on ideas and principles, not individuals
- Use inclusive and professional language
- Accept feedback in good faith

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Political campaigning or partisan attacks
- Harassment, personal attacks, or abusive language
- Discriminatory or exclusionary conduct
- Deliberate misrepresentation of the project’s purpose

Such behavior may result in removal from discussions or contribution
channels at the discretion of the maintainers.

---

## Enforcement

The NGTH Steward(s) are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Enforcement actions are guided by the principles of fairness, proportionality,
and protection of public trust.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including:

- Repository discussions and issues
- Pull requests and reviews
- Any official communication channels associated with NGTH

---

## Reporting

Concerns about behavior may be raised through the organization’s public
contact channels.

Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled with care.
